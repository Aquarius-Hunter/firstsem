Laboratory Courses

Engineering Physics Lab

KAS151P

  • Group A: Newton's rings, Diffraction grating, Polarimeter, Lens combinations, Fiber optic attenuation, He‑Ne laser diffraction/polarization, Bi‑prism, Viscosity measurement, Pendulum (g determination)
  • Group B: Band gap determination, Hall effect, Magnetic field measurement, Stefan's law, Carey Foster bridge, LCR resonance, ECE (copper), Ammeter/Voltmeter calibration, B‑H curve, High resistance measurement

Basic Electrical Engineering Lab

KEE151P

  • Kirchhoff's laws verification, Power factor improvement, RLC resonance analysis, Fluorescent lamp study, Three‑phase power measurement, RLC parameter determination, Transformer load test, DC shunt motor efficiency test, Three‑phase induction motor speed reversal, Cut‑out section demonstrations

Programming Lab

KCS151P

  • Basic I/O, Arithmetic expressions, Conditional statements, Looping constructs, Array manipulations, String operations, Function implementation, Recursive functions, Pointer operations, File handling, Sorting algorithms (Bubble, Insertion, Selection), Searching algorithms (Linear, Binary)

Engineering Graphics Lab

KCE151P

  • Orthographic projections, Isometric views, Sectional views, CAD basics, Machine part drawings, Dimensioning techniques, Assembly drawings, Surface development

10‑Day Intensive Study Plan

STRUCTURED PREPARATION PLAN

Day‑wise Breakdown (Oct 4‑13)

Days 1‑2: Foundation Building

  • • Maths: Matrices & Basic Calculus (6 h)
  • • Programming: Basics & Control Structures (4 h)
  • • Physics: Quantum Mechanics (3 h)
  • • Daily Practice: 20 numerical problems

Days 3‑4: Core Concepts

  • • Maths: Differential Calculus (6 h)
  • • Programming: Arrays & Functions (4 h)
  • • Electrical: DC & AC Circuits (4 h)
  • • Daily Practice: 25 problems + 2 programs

Days 5‑6: Advanced Topics

  • • Maths: Integral & Vector Calculus (7 h)
  • • Programming: Pointers & Files (4 h)
  • • Physics: EM Theory & Optics (4 h)
  • • Environment: Complete syllabus (3 h)
  • • Daily Practice: 30 problems + 3 programs

Days 7‑8: Lab Focus

  • • All Labs: Complete experiments (8 h)
  • • Lab Records: Complete all records (3 h)
  • • Viva Practice: Prepare for questions (2 h)
  • • Revision: Weak topics review (3 h)

Days 9‑10: Final Revision

  • • Full Syllabus: Quick revision (10 h)
  • • Previous Papers: Solve 5 years (4 h)
  • • Mock Tests: Take 2‑3 tests (3 h)
  • • Formula Review: All subjects (2 h)
  • • Relaxation: Proper sleep & stress management

Exam Day Strategy

  • • Wake up early, light breakfast
  • • Revise formulas only (no new topics)
  • • Reach centre 30 min early
  • • Stay calm and confident
